About

    This project has stemmed out of Intel's AI4Youth AI Readiness Programme. I attended the program's first batch in 2020 and learnt about Python and Artificial Intelligence over a span of a couple of months. After the program, I was given the opportunity to be mentored by Ankur Mehta from Intel, and that is when I began working on an algorithm which is now called EvSched.

    I extend sincere gratitude to all the people who supported EvSched and Kronologue by providing their insights about scheduling, and by participating in my survey. This project couldn't have existed without this support.

    The primary purpose of this website is to share this project with everyone around the world, and inch closer towards its basic motto: Making scheduling easier and more effective for everyone, everywhere. I hope to learn from any suggestions that I receive in this process.
